Bio

Hi! I'm Fiona, a passionate researcher dedicated to leveraging the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning for social good, particularly in the field of Ecology.

Prior to this, I obtained my Master's in Computer Science from the University of Washington Bothell and my bachelor’s in Robotics and Automation from PSG College of Technology. I have actively volunteered for a recent social cause using NLP techniques and worked as a machine learning engineer specializing in building multimodal AI systems and developing quantization techniques for neural networks.

I have conducted research that led to pre-print publications on ArXiv and presented a conference paper at the Data for Policy Conference 2022 held in Seattle.
